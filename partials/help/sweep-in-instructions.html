Note

Only the Clear Credit Balance amount in the account will be considered for the Sweep in facility.

One single Savings/Current account can be linked to more than one provider accounts. Incase of unavailable funds in one provider account, funds can be swept in from other provider accounts.

Only the Principal amount of the Fixed Deposit will be considered for the Sweep-in facility.

Fixed Deposit will be broken on Last-in-First-out basis. If there is a lien marked on the Fixed Deposit, please note that the sweep-in will not be maintained.
